You have freedom when you are easy in your harness. 在约束中能感到自如，那你就是自由的了。

Do not do all things you can do, spend all you have, believe all you hear, and tell others all you know. 不要为尽所能为，不要花尽所有，不要全信所闻，不要言尽所知。

Beauty is of soft whisperings. She speaks in our spirit. Her voice yields to our silences like a faint light that quivers in fear of the shadow. 美是温柔的低语，她在我们的心中诉说。她的声音波动在我们的沉默中，犹如一道微弱的光在对阴影的恐惧中颤抖。

Life is a journey instead of a destination. We should pay more attention to the scenery and the feeling along the way. 人生就是一场旅行，不在乎目的地，在乎的应该是沿途的风景以及看风景的心情。

Instead of making a dozen excuses why you can not realize your dreams, just hold to your dream and never give up. 与其找借口解释梦想为什么不能实现，不如坚持你的梦想，永不放弃。

Past is experience, present is experiment, and future is expectation. Use your experience for your experiments to achieve your expectation. 过去是经历，现在是尝试，未来是期待。用经历尝试就会达到你的期待。

Know what you want to do, hold the thought firmly and do every day what should be done, and every sunset will see you that much nearer the goal. 了解你想要做什么，紧紧抓着这个想法并身体力行，每一个黄昏时你会看到自己更接近目标。

A mature person always finds there are less and less people to be blamed. Everybody has his difficulties. 一个成熟的人往往发觉可以责怪的人越来越少，人人都有他的难处。

You know, you never really give up on love. You just get tired and force yourself not to want it. 你知道的，你从没真的放弃了爱。你只是厌倦了，强迫自己不要再想.

Never change yourself just because of other people’s opinions. What makes you different, makes you beautiful! 不要因为别人的看法而改变自己，你的特别之处，令你如此美丽.

Do not regret if you never cherish. Do not hate if you never try to understand. Do not judge until you experience it by yourself. 如果你从不珍惜，就不要后悔；如果你从不试着去理解，就不要憎恨；如果你没有亲自经历过，就不要妄加评论。

Smile and keep being fabulous are the best ways to revenge your haters. They hate to see you like that. 对讨厌你的人最好的反击是，保持微笑和光芒四射，他们最不希望看到这样的你。

Regret for the things we did can be tempered by time; it is regret for the things we did not do that is inconsolable. 为做过的事而后悔会因时间而慢慢平抚，为没做过的事而后悔却是无法平抚。

The days when you feel like giving up are the days you should try the hardest. 那些你觉得要放弃的时候正是你应该加倍努力的时候。

There are differences between giving up and letting go. Giving up is sacrificing what was rightfully yours, while letting go is forgetting what was never yours. 放弃与放手的区别：放弃是牺牲本来属于你的，放手是放下那些从来不是你的。所以放手了就别再遗憾!

Always go with the choice that scares you the most, because that’s the one going to require the most from you.就做那个让你最不安的事情，因为它能发挥你最大的潜能。

Never regret anything. If it’s good, it’s wonderful. If it’s bad, it’s experience. 凡事不必遗憾。若是美好，当做精彩。若是糟糕，当做经历。

Don’t forget the one who helped you. Don’t hate the one who loved you. Don’t cheat the one who believes you. 帮过你的人不要忘，爱过你的人不要恨，信任你的人不要骗。

If you don’t go after what you want, you’ll never have it. If you don’t step forward, you’re always in the same place.不去追逐你所渴求，你将永远不会拥有。不往前走，就将永远停留。

There are no shortcuts to any place worth going. 任何值得去的地方，都没有捷径.

The one who loves you will love your imperfections. The one who does not love you could not understand your beauty. 爱你的人，会爱上你的缺点；不爱你的人，却无法理解你的美。

Use your smile to change the world. Don’t let the world change your smile. 用你的笑容去改变这个世界，别让这个世界改变了你的笑容。

You’re such a child. You think by saying, “I’m sorry,” all the past can be corrected? 你太天真了，你以为说一句“对不起”，过去的一切就一笔勾销了？

I just don’t understand why destiny allowed some people to meet when there’s no way for them to be together.我就是不明白有些人注定没办法在一起，为什么命运还要让他们相遇。

People throw away what they could have by insisting on perfection, which they cannot have, and looking for it where they will never find it. 人们往往坚持完美而扔掉了一些他们原本可以有的东西，但他们是不可能拥有完美的，虽然他们还在永远找不到完美的地方到处搜寻。

Life is full of choices and contradictions, so it is important to learn your heart and try to get the most precious thing in your life. 生活中总是充满了选择和矛盾，请一定审视一下自己的内心，去争取那最珍贵的东西。
